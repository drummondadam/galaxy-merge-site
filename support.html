Frequently asked questions

How do I launch a planet?
Drag any object from the dock at the bottom of the screen out into space, then let go to fling it. Bigger flings travel faster.
How do objects merge?
When two bodies collide they combine, climbing a mass ladder — asteroids and comets build planets and stars, and enough mass collapses into neutron stars and black holes.
What are the ads for?
A couple of special objects (such as the Neutron Star and White Hole) can be unlocked by watching an optional rewarded video ad, which grants several launches. Ads are entirely optional and limited to general-audience content.
Can I lock an object in place?
Tap an object to open its info panel, then use the Lock button to anchor it so you can drag others into it.
Is my data collected?
The game itself collects no personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details, including how advertising works.
